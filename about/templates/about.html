GEIMA


    GEIMA is a newly developed interactive web server for query the Gene expression profile of NASH,Liver cancer,or other disease database from the NCBI.

    The raw data of GEIMA is downloaded from NCBI, and then are computed by a standard pipeline.

    This tool is developed by Yue Yao from IMA Biosys partform.
